  • SecuritE-mail

A Microsoft Outlook plug-in that scans your email for sensitive or classified words that is stored in a database. After scanning the email, the plug-in will return a risk score/level that tells the user how dangerous it is to send the email, but does not prevent the user from sending the email. This project was funded by Rowan University and ASRC Federal Mission Solutions (AFMS).
